There are three ways to load caches into GeoBeagle:

BCaching.com

Once setup, it's by far the easiest: you get updates over the internet! You must be a premium member of Geocaching.com to use this.
  1. Set up a free account on BCaching.com
  2. Tell Geocaching.com to email each Pocket Query to your BCaching.com address
  3. Click "Menu/Settings", and add your BCaching username/password
And then just click "Menu/Sync" to get updates.

Import GPX/LOC/ZIP files

You'll need to download the files to your desktop computer, then mount your phone's sdcard to your computer.
  1. Copy gpx/loc/zip files to the download folder of your sdcard
  2. Click "Menu/Sync"

Search online

You can get cache data "on the fly", by clicking on a link on the geocaching.com web page; only the coordinates are imported. Getting more information by page scraping (as some other apps do) is a violation of geocaching.com's Terms of Service.
  1. Click "Menu/Search Online"
  2. Follow the instructions on that page
